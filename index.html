Product design, development

Cocco Panel

Product design, development

Cocco Panel

Problem Statement:

Cocco is a Colombian brand of children's accessories. Its main problem was the lack of software that could track its technical specifications and the cost of its materials for production.

Requirements:

The application requirements were as follows:

  • Develop a login with role permission control.
  • Develop a record of materials used.
  • Develop a record of products with technical sheets and files necessary for production, such as molds, images, and designs.

UX and product design role:

My role as a UX designer was to conduct research on the current functioning of the company's data sheets and prototype custom software, the development stage of which was carried out and validated under my supervision.

Product Design, development

Astrocube Network

Product Design, development

Astrocube Network

Problem statement:

Astrocube Network was an online community for a video game that faced several challenges with its in-game experience. Their greatest need was an intuitive, easy-to-use website that would complement their in-game experience.

The main requirements of the project were the following:

  • Being able to log in to the platform with the same account as on the web.
  • Have a group and permission management system linked to in-game permissions.
  • Have a synchronized in-game penalty and appeal management system.
  • Have access to a forum integrated with the platform.
  • Have a store integrated with the platform.
  • Each user has their profile that reflects basic in-game information..

My role in the project:

My main role in the development of this platform was the complete creation of the web platform (named Polaris) and the backend (named Perseus) that supported the platform.

In-Game Supervision and Development:

Along with this, my supervision of the in-game development team was also essential, creating the necessary tools that would allow communication between the game platform and the web platform, allowing the product to acquire its functional value in record time, having supervised and directly participated in the in-game modifications.

Concept Design, development

Animentor

Concept Design, development

Animentor

What is Animentor?:

Animentor is a product I designed for animation students who lack visual references for certain actions.

How does it work?:

Thanks to augmented reality technology and the LiDAR sensor included in the latest generation of iPhones at the time, people can record themselves, and Animentor will automatically motion track a skeleton, whose movements can then be exported to an animation program like Blender.

Development, project management

Gobernemos

Development, project management

Gobernemos

What is Gobernemos?

Gobernemos is a platform developed to promote political participation in Colombia. The original design idea was validated as a mobile app from which people could create requests that can be discussed, commented on, and voted on. Those ideas that are most popular can be evaluated by a council member or a representative of the governor's office.

Development role:

In the development of this application, my role was important in guiding and supervising the development team for the proper implementation of all features. However, I also played a direct role in the development of this application, playing a key role in the constant evaluation of code quality and merging complex features to work seamlessly with the main branch of the app's development.

Concept Design, UI design

Orvibo Revolution

Concept Design, UI design

Orvibo Revolution

Orvibo Revolution is a product concept inspired by the Orvibo brand and developed as a business model that seeks to unite devices from other manufacturers with Orvibo's Smart Home AI ecosystem.

For the development of this concept I implemented three proposals:

Website

Aplicativo web donde se muestra cómo funciona Orvibo Revolution, junto a una lista de proveedores compatibles y soporte para configuración.

Mobile App

An app that functions as a successor to Home AI, designed as a smart home app that provides compatibility with all Google Home or Apple Home Kit devices.

Revolution Go!

An educational app that allows people to learn how to create their own home with Orvibo devices, in addition to creating a gallery with photos from each person who uses the app.
